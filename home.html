OUR CLASSES

Yoga

Join our yoga classes to improve flexibility and mindfulness.

Come give yourself a break from your busy schedule and join our yoga classes.

Yoga is a holistic practice that focuses on physical poses, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques.

Our experienced instructors will guide you through various yoga poses to improve flexibility, strength, and mental well-being.

Zumba

Love dance and want to stay fit? Join our Zumba classes!

Zumba is a high-energy dance fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance moves.

Our enthusiastic instructors will lead you through fun and dynamic routines that will help you burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and boost your mood.

Stretches

Strengthen and tone your body with our stretches class.

Stretches are essential for maintaining flexibility, preventing injuries, and improving overall mobility.

Our stretches class includes a variety of stretching exercises targeting different muscle groups to help you feel more relaxed and energized.
